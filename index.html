About Us

Random Makethon of Kindness

Tech@NYU is a community passionate builders, programmers and entrepreneurs that hope to make the world a better place by one step at a time.

In order to accomplish this, we are hosting a makerthon for kids (9 to 15 year olds) to provide a fun way to get the younger generation into creating their own cool graphics, websites, and even games! The theme of the makerthon is based on being random and kind to anyone and everyone! We really see the value in not only enabling youth to create cool things, but encouraging them to express themselves in kind and random ways.

During the event, highly skilled and professional mentors will help guide them to creating a complete project that they never knew they could make.

The makerthon will be at the NYU Kimmel Center for University Life on November 21st with 7 hours (9:00AM to 4:00PM) of workshops to teach them what they want to learn and build.
