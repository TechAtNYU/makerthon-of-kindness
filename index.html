About

Hello!

Tech@NYU, a tech and entrepreneurship student body, is planning a small programming day for kids called Random Makerthon of Kindness.

The event will be a small hackathon for youth (9 to 15 year olds) who are interested in programming and want to program something random and fun with the guidance of various mentors. Random Makerthon of Kindess will last 12 hours on November 21st and will take place in the Leslie eLab. Currently we are seeking volunteers for helping the event and participants for the event.

Learn

Are you interested in learning something new, fun and useful in order to create something random, cool and awesome?

Well rest assured, our makerthon is the right place for you.

Here are some cool resources to get you started on programming:

  • Learn Scratch!
  • Learn to program with CodeAcademy
  • Build games with Processing!
  • Build games with Unity!
